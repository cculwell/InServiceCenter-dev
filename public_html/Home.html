Building / Reservation Information

The Athens State AMSTI/In-Service Center is comprised of offices for the In-Service Center, AMSTI, ASIM, and TIM, as well as the AMSTI warehouse and 3 training rooms. These rooms are available for our systems and the ALSDE to host LEA professional development and meetings. Our three meeting rooms are equipped with interactive boards, projectors, document cameras, and tables and chairs. The room capacities are all approximate, and are as follows. Room A, located at the front of the building accommodates up to 28 people at tables and chairs. Room B, in the rear of the building, will accommodate up to 32 people at tables and chairs. Room C, in the rear of the building, will accommodate up to 60 at tables and chairs. Please view the calendar for available dates, and complete the form for room reservation requests.
