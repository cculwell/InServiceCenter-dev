ASRIC Governing Board Information

The Athens State Regional In-Service Center’s governing board is comprised of eight classroom teachers, four administrators, two higher education representatives, one school board member from one of our 10 systems, and an Alabama State Department Representative.

Interested in serving the board and assisting with selecting professional development policies, and helping choose the path our Regional In-Service Center is taking? Contact us!

We are filling the following vacancies on the board before the beginning of the 2017-2018 school year: two teachers, large system; and an administrator and teacher, small system.
