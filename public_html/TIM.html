Technology in Motion

Technology in Motion is a governor’s initiative designed to bring professional development in the area of technology to in-service teachers. The program at Athens State University’s Regional In-Service Center began in 2000 and has provided on-site training to hundreds of teachers. The program is currently under the supervision of the Office of Technology Initiatives in Montgomery.
